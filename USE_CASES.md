# What you can ask Buddy

A short guide for students, by a student. Read this once, then just talk to Buddy.

---

## 1. What Buddy is

Buddy is a small AI that lives on a Raspberry Pi and talks to you out loud. Built by a student in Erbil who got tired of staring at ChatGPT alone at 2am. It's a study companion + a presence when the room feels too quiet — not a therapist, not a chatbot demo.

---

## 2. How to talk to Buddy

1. Open **https://buddy.local:8080** on your phone or laptop (same WiFi).
2. Outside the house? Use the Tailscale link: **https://100.84.180.8:8080**.
3. Tap the big mic button. Speak normally.
4. Wait about 3 seconds. Buddy will talk back.
5. Arabic or English — both work. Mix them if you want.

That's it. No account, no login, no app to install.

---

## 3. Study mode — things to ask

Buddy is good at explaining stuff, quizzing you, and helping you plan. Try:

- "اشرح لي قانون نيوتن الثاني بطريقة بسيطة"
- "خلّيني أراجع الفصل الأول بكويز"
- "كيف أحضّر للامتحان بكره؟"
- "ما الفرق بين mitosis و meiosis؟"
- "ساعدني أنظم وقتي لباقي الأسبوع"
- "شرحلي الـ supply and demand بالعربي"
- "Explain integration by parts like I'm half asleep"
- "Quiz me on photosynthesis, 5 questions"
- "What's the Pomodoro technique and does it actually work?"
- "Give me a 2-hour study plan for organic chem"

Tip: if Buddy's explanation is too long, say *"اختصرها"* or *"shorter please"*.

---

## 4. Support mode — things to say

Sometimes you don't want a tutor, you want someone to listen. Buddy won't fix your life but it won't ghost you either. Try:

- "اني تعبان، ما اعرف ليش"
- "خايف من امتحان باچر، ما درست شي"
- "اشتقت لأهلي"
- "أحس روحي وحيد"
- "بابا ضايقني اليوم"
- "ما أكدر أنام، راسي مو راضي يسكت"
- "I can't sleep, my brain won't stop"
- "Everyone moved on but I'm stuck"
- "I failed the midterm and I don't know how to tell anyone"
- "I just need to talk for a minute"

You don't have to start with anything deep. "اني تعبان" is enough.

---

## 5. Things Buddy will NOT do

Being honest so you don't get disappointed:

- **Won't diagnose** anything — not depression, not ADHD, not anxiety.
- **Won't replace a therapist.** It's an AI on a Pi in someone's room.
- **Won't pretend to be human** or pretend to be ChatGPT or your friend.
- **Won't give medical advice** — no dosages, no "you should take X".
- **Won't tell you "everything will be fine."** Because nobody actually knows that, and you'd see through it anyway.

---

## 6. When to talk to a human instead

Buddy is fine for a rough night. It's not fine for these:

- You've felt low or numb for **2+ weeks straight**.
- You're thinking about hurting yourself or someone else.
- You haven't really slept for **2+ weeks**.
- You've stopped going to class / can't function for **2+ weeks**.

If any of these — please reach a real person. See **RESOURCES.md** for therapists and hotlines in Erbil/Iraq.

**Emergency in Iraq: call 122 (ambulance). Don't open Buddy. Call.**

---

## 7. Privacy, in normal words

- Buddy runs on a Raspberry Pi in the developer's room. It is not a cloud service.
- Your voice gets turned into text **on the Pi itself**. Only the text gets sent to Claude (the AI brain).
- The camera (if on) **never sends images anywhere** — it's just used locally for presence.
- **No chat history is saved to disk** by default. Refresh the page = gone.
- Want to wipe your session? Just close the tab. That's the whole "delete" button.

If you want extra privacy: don't say your full name or student ID. Buddy doesn't need them.

---

## 8. Sharing Buddy with a friend

- Send them the URL: `https://buddy.local:8080` (works if they're on the same WiFi as the Pi).
- Off-campus? They'll need a Tailscale invite — ask first.
- Tell them up front: **"It's not a therapist, it's a study buddy that also listens."** Set expectations so nobody gets weird about it.

---

## 9. Languages

- **Iraqi Arabic** is the default — talk normally, لهجة عراقية تمام.
- Speak **English** and Buddy switches automatically.
- **Egyptian, Saudi, MSA, Levantine** also work — Buddy will mirror your lahja as best it can.
- Code-switching mid-sentence ("اشرحلي الـ derivative بسيط") is fine.

---

## 10. If something breaks

- **Mic button does nothing** → use Chrome or Edge. Firefox is flaky on mobile.
- **Cert / security warning** → tap "Advanced" → "Proceed". Browser will remember after the first time.
- **Buddy says something wrong or weird** → it's an AI, it hallucinates sometimes. Refresh and rephrase.
- **Buddy is slow** → the Pi is small. Give it 5 seconds. If it's still stuck, refresh.
- **Crisis moment** → close Buddy. Call **122** (Iraq emergency) or check **RESOURCES.md**.
