## 1. Crisis Hotlines / خطوط الأزمات

### Iraq / Kurdistan
| Service | Number | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Ambulance / إسعاف** | **122** | Nationwide. Use for any medical emergency including a suicide attempt or overdose. |
| **Police / شرطة** | **104** | Nationwide. |
| **Civil Defense / دفاع مدني** | **115** | Fire / rescue. |

*Note: Iraq does **not** have a single dedicated, well-publicised mental-health crisis hotline equivalent to 988 (US) or 116 123 (UK). Some online sources mention 122 as a "mental-health line," but this is **the general ambulance number** — call it for medical emergencies. For non-emergency mental-health support use the NGOs below.* **[VERIFY before using as a mental-health hotline]**

### KRG Ministry of Health mental-health hotline
- No publicly verified dedicated KRG MoH mental-health hotline could be confirmed at time of writing. **[VERIFY — ask at any public hospital or call 122 for direction]**

## 2. University Counselling at Erbil Universities / الإرشاد النفسي في جامعات أربيل

Verifiable, public-facing student-counselling centres are **rare** at Erbil universities. In most cases the right first step is the **Student Affairs office (شؤون الطلبة)** of the specific college.

| University | Status |
|---|---|
| **Salahaddin University–Erbil (SUE)** | No public-facing dedicated student counselling centre confirmed. → **Check the Vice-Presidency for Student Affairs** at su.edu.krd. |
| **American University of Kurdistan (AUK), Duhok** | Public sources do not confirm a named "Dr. Amel Counseling Center." → **Check the Office of Student Affairs / Dean of Students** at auk.edu.krd. |
| **Cihan University–Erbil** | Offers academic advising and an on-campus medical clinic; no public dedicated psychological-counselling centre confirmed. → Email info@cihanuniversity.edu.iq or visit the Student Affairs office. |
| **Tishk International University (formerly Ishik) / Lebanese French University (LFU) / others** | No verified public counselling-centre listing. → **Check Student Affairs office in person.** |

**General advice for students:** ask Student Affairs (شؤون الطلبة) or your college dean's office for a referral. Many faculties have a *Psychology Department* with staff who can refer you to a clinician even if no formal counselling centre exists.

## 3. Anonymous Online Support / دعم مجهول عبر الإنترنت

- **7 Cups** (https://www.7cups.com) — free 24/7 anonymous chat with trained volunteer listeners; community forums; English primarily, some other languages. Free tier is genuinely free; therapy tier is paid.
- **Befrienders Worldwide — Arabic portal:** https://befrienders.org/foreign-language/members/arabic/
- **Takalam** (https://takalamhere.com) — UAE-based Arabic + English online counselling platform with licensed counsellors and an Arabic AI companion ("Aila"). Counselling sessions are **paid**; some self-help tools and AI companion are free. Good option for Arabic-speaking students who want professional help online.
- **Arab Therapy** (https://arabtherapy.com) — book online sessions with Arabic-speaking licensed therapists. **Paid.**

## 4. Self-Help Techniques / تقنيات المساعدة الذاتية

Quick tools when you feel overwhelmed. Not a substitute for professional care.

**4-7-8 Breathing / تنفّس 4-7-8**
- EN: Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale slowly through your mouth for 8. Repeat 3–4 times.
- AR: استنشق من أنفك 4 ثوانٍ، احبس النفس 7 ثوانٍ، ثم ازفر ببطء من فمك 8 ثوانٍ. كرّر 3-4 مرات.

**5-4-3-2-1 Grounding / تقنية التأريض 5-4-3-2-1**
- EN: Name **5** things you can see, **4** you can touch, **3** you can hear, **2** you can smell, **1** you can taste. Brings you back to the present.
- AR: سمِّ **5** أشياء تراها، **4** تلمسها، **3** تسمعها، **2** تشمّها، **1** تتذوّقها. يُعيدك إلى اللحظة الحاضرة.

**Body Scan / مسح الجسد**
- EN: Slowly move attention from your toes up to your head, noticing tension without judgement; relax each area as you pass. 3–5 minutes.
- AR: حرّك انتباهك ببطء من أصابع قدميك إلى رأسك، لاحظ التوتر دون حكم، وأرخِ كل منطقة. 3-5 دقائق.

**Worry Window / نافذة القلق**
- EN: Pick a fixed 15-minute "worry slot" later in the day. When a worry shows up now, write it down and tell yourself "I'll think about this at 7 pm." It trains your brain to defer rumination.
- AR: حدِّد فترة 15 دقيقة لاحقاً في اليوم كـ"نافذة قلق". عندما يظهر قلقٌ الآن، اكتبه وقل لنفسك "سأفكر فيه الساعة 7 مساءً". هذا يدرّب الدماغ على تأجيل الاجترار.

**Short Walk + Sun / مشي قصير وضوء الشمس**
- EN: A 10–15 minute walk outside in daylight lifts mood, improves sleep, and lowers anxiety. Even on a study deadline.
- AR: المشي 10-15 دقيقة في ضوء النهار يُحسِّن المزاج والنوم ويقلّل القلق. حتى أثناء مواعيد التسليم.

## 5. When to Seek Immediate Help / متى تطلب مساعدة فورية

Call **122** (ambulance) or go to the nearest emergency room **right now** if you experience any of:

- EN: Thoughts of ending your life, or a plan / means to do so.
- AR: أفكار بإنهاء حياتك أو خطة أو وسيلة للقيام بذلك.
- EN: Hurting yourself, or strong urges to hurt yourself or others.
- AR: إيذاء نفسك أو رغبة قوية في إيذاء نفسك أو الآخرين.
- EN: Hearing voices, seeing things others don't, or feeling completely disconnected from reality.
- AR: سماع أصوات أو رؤية أشياء لا يراها غيرك، أو الشعور بانفصال تام عن الواقع.
- EN: Cannot eat, sleep, or function for more than several days.
- AR: عدم القدرة على الأكل أو النوم أو القيام بأعمالك اليومية لأكثر من عدة أيام.
- EN: After a traumatic event you feel numb, panicked, or detached for hours.
- AR: بعد حدث صادم تشعر بالخدر أو الذعر أو الانفصال لساعات.
- EN: Heavy use of alcohol, pills, or drugs to cope.
- AR: استخدام مكثّف للكحول أو الأدوية أو المخدرات للتعامل مع المشاعر.

**If a friend tells you they want to die, take it seriously. Stay with them and call 122 together.**
**إذا قال لك صديقك إنه يريد أن يموت، خذ الأمر على محمل الجد. ابقَ معه واتصلوا بـ 122 معاً.**

## 6. Stigma & Cultural Notes / ملاحظات حول الوصمة الاجتماعية

Mental-health stigma is real in the Kurdish and wider Iraqi context — many people worry about family reputation, gossip, or being seen as "weak." This is changing, but slowly. Asking for help is **a strength, not a weakness** — it takes more courage to face a problem than to hide from it. (في الثقافة الكردية والعراقية، طلب المساعدة النفسية ليس ضعفاً بل شجاعة؛ من حقّك أن تطلب الدعم بسرّية تامة.)

If you are not ready to talk to family, an **anonymous online service** (7 Cups, Befrienders Arabic, Takalam) or a **trusted faculty member** can be a good first step. Speaking to one professional does **not** put anything on a public record visible to your family.
